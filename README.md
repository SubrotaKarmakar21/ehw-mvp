# Elevate Health World (MVP)

Elevate Health World is a healthcare coordination platform that builds AI-integrated smart clinics through a franchise model. 
It connects patients to a verified network of hospitals, pharmacies, and diagnostic centers via a unified digital dashboard.
It's mission is to build the infrastructure to modernize traditional practices into tech-enabled, standardized medical hubs.

Elevate Health Dashboard (for clinics and doctors) is a healthcare platform designed to connect clinics, doctors, and patients through a unified digital system.

---

## Features

- Patient Management System 
- Doctor Management System
- Clinic Service Management and Tracking 
- Appointment Booking
- Prescription Generation and Management 
- Billing System
- User Authentication (Login / Registration)

---

## Problem We Are Solving

Majority of the clinics in India still operate manually or with fragmented systems.

This leads to:
- Poor patient record management
- Inefficient workflows
- Lack of digital coordination
- Data loss and choas

---

## Our Solution

Elevate Health World provides:
- A centralized clinic management system 
- Digital patient records
- Streamlined appointment & billing processes
- Digital prescription system with AI added features
- Secured data syncing with admins, staffs and doctors to manage operaions smoothly
- Scalable infrastructure for clinic networks
